Sultan Bin Saeed Al Mansouri

"I welcome you to a journey of success: An account of international achievements by more than 130 companies that were born in the UAE with a global vision. In this book, you can find stories that were engineered with persistence, entrepreneurial spirit, innovation and superior customer-centricity. The book showcases to the world examples of Emirati companies that export the essence of the UAE's model and experience that, in 42 years, created a globally pioneering nation, namely mastery.

Perhaps what’s unique about the UAE is its understanding that the private sector is an essential partner in achieving the country's vision of becoming a globally-competitive nation in every field. It’s from this notion that the government has always embraced a strategy of supporting the establishment of leading companies in every key economic sector. Through easing business setup procedures, creating the right infrastructure and connectivity locally and globally, fostering excellent relations with other nations and the carefully planned international promotion of "brand UAE”, the Government has supported this group of "Highflyers" and many other UAE companies with an embedded international strategy. Today, with international investments that exceed USD 90 billion that span several economic sectors in more than 100 countries, the UAE private sector is committed to delivering to the world the best of its accumulated management, marketing and operations expertise. Simultaneously, acquiring and bringing to the UAE new capacities that will help in fostering the growth of our thriving economy.

With the wise vision and guidance of our enlightened leadership, the Ministry of Economy has and will always continue to support the international expansion plans of our national companies through well-studied trade and investment policies, international agreements, promotional roadshows and the establishment of the Council of Emirati companies investing abroad.

Through this book, we highlight the pivotal role of the UAE Highflyers in responding to the needs of less developed economies and in contributing to the sustainable development of our thriving economy.

I thank you all and wish you a pleasant journey through the success stories."

The Initiative

Back Home
The Ministry of Economy, along with Investsource, have launched the Highflyers initiative with the following objectives:
  • Presenting to the world examples of case studies of how companies in the UAE have been built and grown to be at par with major international companies in their exhibition.
  • Providing a tool for UAE companies to reach key decision makers and to be considered as preferred partners by international companies.
  • Creating a community of successful companies that can in group present a brand UAE via different media. The brand presentation will come through shared value and joint promotion.
  • Constructing a platform where the companies can visually tell and show their success stories for the first time and using a unique and cost effective means.

The HIGHFLYERS are UAE-based companies that:

  • Have utilized, with entrepreneurial spirit, diligence, persistence and innovation to build a winning organization.
  • Developed their products and services based on the highest standards of quality and continuously improved their offerings using superior innovation.
  • Shaped their people and products to be ambassadors of Brand UAE: customer-oriented service, innovation, openness, and the drive of excellence.
  • Successfully utilized their resources and created the strategic partnerships and alliances required to strongly enter the international market with well planned investments.
  • Generated positive impact on the economies and societies of host countries through responsible contributions and investment decisions.
  • Have been recognized and awarded locally and internationally for their achievements and excellence in their respective sectors.

Key Forum Topics

In addition to celebrating the achievements of the Highflyers Companies and addressing the dynamic challenges faced when entering a new market, some of the other key issues that will be discussed at The Highflyers Forum are:

  • Overcoming the different challenges and the cooperation with the governmental entities around the different regions.
  • The impact of changing political and economic conditions on the operations and investments of the Highflyers companies and the government’s actions towards reducing the negative influence.
  • The changes on the international expansion strategies of the UAE Highflyers given the current global economic and financial conditions.
  • The embarking of new promotion strategies by emerging markets and how effective in increasing investment flow from the UAE.
  • The importance of establishing investment support mechanisms to ease the market entry for the UAE companies in new promising markets.
